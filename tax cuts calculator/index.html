The typical Federal employee will lose $300,000-$900,000, but exact financial impact differs greatly from one individual to another.

In May the White House released its a full budget proposal which calls for major cuts to the retirement benefits of every federal employee, including civilian workers and the armed forces. At first glance, this certainly seems like some pretty bad news for federal workers hoping to receive their previously agreed upon income in retirement. But absent some hard data it may be unclear just how much this will impact any given individual.
